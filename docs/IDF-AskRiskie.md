### 4. Description of Invention

AskRiskie is a browser-based, AI-enabled Software as a Service (SaaS) platform that consolidates the full risk management lifecycle for medical device manufacturers into a single unified system, with compliance coverage across safety, AI/ML, cybersecurity, environmental, supply chain, and M&A risk domains.

**Core architecture:**

- **Unified risk register** — safety hazards, security threats, and AI/ML failure modes are managed in a single interconnected register, enabling cross-domain risk propagation analysis (e.g., a cybersecurity vulnerability flagged as a safety hazard automatically)
- **AI-enabled risk assessment** — AI-assisted identification, estimation, and evaluation of risks across all domains
- **Human in the Loop review** — structured clinician/expert review checkpoints throughout the risk lifecycle, preserving human accountability in AI-assisted decisions
- **Evidence engine** — automated evidence retrieval from FDA MAUDE, EUDAMED, and published scientific literature to support risk acceptability decisions
- **Multi-domain compliance coverage:**
  - ISO 14971 safety risk management
  - AI Act / AI governance risk evaluation
  - Cybersecurity threat modelling (STRIDE, MITRE ATT&CK)
  - Environmental / ecodesign compliance
  - Supply chain risk tracking
  - M&A due diligence risk assessment

**Deployment model:** Cloud-based SaaS with three tiers — Starter (€149/month), Growth (€399/month+), and Enterprise (custom). Thirty-day free trial. Targets medical device manufacturers from SME to multi-site enterprise, regulatory professionals, and acquisition teams.

### 5. Why is this invention more advantageous than present technology? What are its novel or unusual features? What problems does it solve?

**Problem:** Medical device risk management is fragmented. Safety risk (ISO 14971), AI/ML risk (AI Act, ISO/IEC 42001), cybersecurity risk (IEC 81001-5-1, NIST), and supply chain risk are typically managed in separate, disconnected files and tools. This creates blind spots — particularly at the intersection of cybersecurity and safety, where a security vulnerability can become a patient safety hazard. No existing commercial platform unifies all these domains with AI assistance and evidence retrieval in a single register.

**Novel features:**

- **Cross-domain risk propagation:** the only platform that treats safety, AI/ML, and cybersecurity risks as interconnected — a change in one domain automatically flags implications in others
- **Evidence engine with live regulatory database integration:** automated pull from FDA MAUDE adverse event data, EUDAMED, and peer-reviewed literature, directly linked to individual risk assessments — no existing tool offers this in a unified risk management workflow
- **Human in the Loop architecture applied to regulatory compliance:** structured human review checkpoints embedded in an AI-assisted risk workflow, satisfying both AI Act accountability requirements and ISO 14971's requirement for qualified human review
- **M&A due diligence module:** risk assessment scoped for acquisition workflows — unique in the medical device regulatory tooling market
- **Regulatory depth across multiple frameworks simultaneously:** a single platform covers ISO 14971, IEC 62304, EU MDR, AI Act, IEC 81001-5-1, and ecodesign requirements — no competitor covers this breadth in one tool
- **Scalable from SME to enterprise:** self-serve entry tier accessible to small manufacturers without a full regulatory affairs team; enterprise tier supports multi-site, multi-product portfolios

### 8. What is the potential commercial application of this invention?

AskRiskie is a subscription SaaS platform targeting:

- **Medical device manufacturers** (SME to enterprise) requiring ISO 14971 risk management, AI Act compliance, and cybersecurity risk governance
- **Regulatory affairs professionals** and consultants managing risk files across multiple clients
- **Corporate acquisition teams** conducting M&A due diligence on medical device companies

Current pricing: Starter €149/month, Growth €399/month+, Enterprise by arrangement. The platform is live at askriskie.com with a 30-day free trial.

The total addressable market includes approximately 50,000 medical device manufacturers operating under EU MDR and equivalent international frameworks, with growing demand driven by the AI Act and increasing cybersecurity regulatory requirements.

**Founding customer pipeline:** MemoryTell Ltd. has been informally advised they may use AskRiskie for risk management in the course of their SaMD development. This arrangement is being formalised as a founding customer commercial licence (product and security risk features), replacing the informal arrangement. MemoryTell Ltd. would represent an early reference customer in the medical device SaMD segment.
